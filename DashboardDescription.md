# Dashboard Overview

This Power BI dashboard was designed to provide business stakeholders with a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer activity, and revenue trends.

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Summary
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Total Customers

### Sales Performance
- Monthly Revenue Trends
- Revenue by Region
- Revenue by Product

### Product Analysis
- Top Selling Products
- Category Performance
- Product Revenue Contribution

### Customer Insights
- Top Customers
- Customer Purchase Patterns
- Regional Customer Distribution

## Business Questions Answered

1. Which products generate the highest revenue?
2. Which regions have the strongest sales performance?
3. How does revenue change month-over-month?
4. Who are the highest-value customers?
5. Which product categories contribute most to revenue?

## Key Findings

- Electronics generated the highest overall revenue.
- Toronto and Mississauga produced the largest share of sales.
- Laptop sales accounted for a significant portion of total revenue.
- Revenue showed consistent growth throughout the reporting period.
